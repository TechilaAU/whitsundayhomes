Common Questions

What people usually ask

What does "fixed price" actually mean?

The price in your contract is the price you pay — regardless of material costs, labour, or weather delays. We absorb cost variations. You don't.

How long does the whole process take?

From first consultation to handover, most builds take 9–12 months including approvals (4–6 weeks), pre-construction (2–4 weeks), and construction (20–28 weeks).

Do I need land before contacting you?

No — we can help you find suitable land or offer house and land packages. Talk to us about what you're looking for and we'll point you in the right direction.

Can I modify one of your standard designs?

Yes. Every design can be modified — room sizes, layout, facade, garage bays. We'll let you know how modifications affect the price before you commit to anything.

What are site costs and are they included?

Site costs cover earthworks, soil testing, retaining walls, and service connections. These are site-specific and assessed separately. We include a site cost estimate in your quote after our site visit.

Do you build on sloping blocks?

Yes. We build on flat, sloping, and steep blocks. Sloping blocks may require additional earthworks or a split-level design approach — all factored into your fixed price quote.

What warranty do I get on my new home?

QBCC statutory warranty: 1 year for non-structural defects, 3 years for major defects, 6 years 6 months for structural defects — all backed by QBCC Home Warranty Insurance.

Can I visit the site during construction?

Absolutely. You have full site access throughout construction. We just ask that you check in with Mike or James first so we can make sure the site is safe for your visit.
