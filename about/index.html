Our Story

A family business built on experience

Whitsunday Homes is a family-owned building company founded by the Bird family — former Perth builders who relocated to Queensland and built a portfolio spanning three building companies and over 100 residential and commercial projects.

We brought that experience to the Whitsunday region because we saw an opportunity to do things differently — a builder who combines genuine construction expertise with personal service. Led by licensed builder Dave Bird with 15+ years of experience, we take on a limited number of projects each year so every client gets the attention they deserve.

We're not a volume builder. Every home is treated like it's our own — because this is a family business, and our name is on every one of them.

How We Work

Three things we never compromise on

01
Honesty in every conversation

We tell you what things actually cost, what the realistic timeline is, and what to watch out for. No spin, no upselling, no vanishing once the contract is signed.

02
Fixed pricing without exceptions

Every build is fixed price. We absorb cost overruns — you don't. That's the deal, and we stand by it on every single project we take on.

Local knowledge no one else has

We know cyclone ratings, soil classifications, council requirements and what actually works in tropical Queensland. That knowledge saves you money and prevents problems.
