Build Process

How it works

From first conversation to handing you the keys — a clear, stress-free process every step of the way.

01
Choose Your Design

Browse our range of plans or bring your own ideas. We'll help you find the right design for your block and budget.

02
Site Assessment

We assess your land, soil type, slope and orientation to tailor the build to your specific site conditions.

03
Fixed Price Contract

We provide a fully itemised fixed-price contract. No hidden costs. What you sign is what you pay.

04
Build & Handover

Construction begins with regular updates throughout. We hand over the keys to your finished home on schedule.
