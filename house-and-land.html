How It Works

From enquiry to handover

01
Choose your package

Browse available packages and register interest. We'll send you full details including land size, block orientation, and included design specifications.

02
One contract signed

A single QBCC-compliant contract covers both the land purchase and fixed-price build. Your finance is arranged on the combined package price.

03
Colour selections

While approvals are processed, you attend a personalisation appointment to select colours, fixtures, and finishes from our standard inclusions range.

04
Keys at handover

Construction completes and we hand over the keys. Your new home is registered in your name, fully inspected and certified, ready to move in or rent.
