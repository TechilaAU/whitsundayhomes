Building in Proserpine

What you need to know

Site & terrain

Proserpine sits in the Pioneer Valley with predominantly flat terrain — ideal for single-storey designs and acreage builds. Larger block sizes mean more flexibility with design orientation, shed placement, and landscaping. Soil classifications vary across the township; we include a soil test in every Proserpine site assessment.

Council & planning

Proserpine sits within the Whitsunday Regional Council planning area. Rural residential and acreage lots may require additional assessments for bushfire overlay or rural zone setbacks. We're experienced with both township and rural builds in this area.
