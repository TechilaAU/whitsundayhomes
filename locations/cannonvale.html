Building in Cannonvale

What you need to know

Site & terrain

Cannonvale's terrain is generally the most build-friendly in the Whitsunday region — predominantly flat to gently sloping with well-serviced estates. Site costs are typically lower than Airlie Beach, and the established street infrastructure means most blocks have easy access to all services.

Council & planning

Most Cannonvale residential estates fall within low or medium residential zones under the Whitsunday Regional Council Planning Scheme. Development approval is typically straightforward for standard residential builds, and we handle the full DA process on your behalf.
